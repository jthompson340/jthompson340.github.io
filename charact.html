Edward Cullen

A vampire who lives with his adoptive family in Forks, Washington. Despite his immortal nature, Edward becomes infatuated with Bella Swan, a human.

Played by Robert Pattinson

Bella Swan

A human teenager who moves to Forks, Washington, to live with her father. She soon becomes captivated by Edward Cullen, a vampire.

Played by Kristen Stewart

Jacob Black

A werewolf who is in love with Bella.

Played by Taylor Lautner

Alice Cullen

Edward's sister who can see the future.

Played by Ashley Greene

Carlisle Cullen

The compassionate 370-year-old, vampire patriarch of the Cullen family.

Played by Peter Facinelli

Renée Dwyer

Bella's free-spirited and eccentric mother.

Played by Sarah Clarke

Esme Cullen

The loving and supportive vampire matriarch of the Cullen family.

Played by Elizabeth Reaser

Emmett Cullen

The strongest vampire in the Cullen family.

Played by Kellan Lutz

Rosalie Hale

Edward's adoptive sister, known for her beauty and elegance.

Played by Nikki Reed

Jasper Hale

Edward's adoptive brother, who can manipulate emotions.

Played by Jackson Rathbone

Charlie Swan

Bella's father and Forks’ Chief of Police.

Played by Billy Burke

James

A nomadic vampire with incredible hunting instincts, he becomes obsessed with tracking down Bella.
